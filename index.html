🧭 La chuleta del zoom

IN · Plano general (contenedores)
Tiempo: años y meses (in 2026, in July), estaciones (in summer), décadas y siglos (in the 1990s), partes del día (in the morning), plazos = "dentro de" (in ten minutes).
Lugar: países y ciudades (in Spain), habitaciones y espacios cerrados (in the kitchen, in the car, in bed), zonas (in the mountains, in the countryside), papel e imágenes (in the newspaper, in a photo).
ON · Plano medio (superficies y líneas)
Tiempo: días (on Monday), fechas (on July 5th), días señalados (on my birthday, on Christmas Day), día + parte (on Monday mornings).
Lugar: calles y carreteras (on Calle Mayor, on the motorway), superficies (on the table, on the wall, on the roof), plantas (on the second floor), transporte grande o que se monta (on the bus, on a bike, on foot), islas y costas (on an island, on the coast), pantallas y listas (on TV, on the internet, on the list, on page 12).
AT · Primer plano (puntos exactos)
Tiempo: horas (at 9pm), instantes (at noon, at midnight, at dawn), fijas (at night, at the weekend, at Christmas, at the age of 17).
Lugar: puntos exactos (at the bus stop, at number 25, at the door, at the traffic lights), actividades (at work, at home, at school, at university), eventos (at a party, at a concert), servicios (at reception).
Ø · Sin preposición
Con next / last / this / every y con today / tomorrow / yesterday no se usa preposición: see you next Monday, I saw her last night, call me tomorrow morning.

⚔️ Contrastes letales (donde caen hasta los avanzados)

in time ≠ on time
'In time' = a tiempo, antes de que sea tarde. 'On time' = puntual, a la hora exacta.
in the end ≠ at the end (of)
'In the end' = finalmente, después de todo. 'At the end of X' = en el punto final de algo concreto.
at the moment ≠ in a moment
'At the moment' = ahora mismo. 'In a moment' = dentro de un momento.
in the corner ≠ on the corner
IN = el rincón interior de una habitación. ON/AT = la esquina exterior de la calle.
in the back of the car ≠ at the back of the room
Dentro de un vehículo: IN. Parte trasera de una sala o espacio: AT.
arrive in Madrid ≠ arrive at the hotel
Llegar a ciudades y países: IN. Llegar a edificios y puntos: AT.
in the morning ≠ at night ≠ on Monday morning
Partes del día: IN. 'Night' es excepción fija: AT. Y si nombras el día, el día manda: ON.
in the newspaper ≠ on the internet
Papel impreso: IN. Pantallas y emisiones: ON (on TV, on the radio, on a website).
you look great in this photo
Las personas salen DENTRO de la imagen: IN, aunque la foto parezca una superficie.
